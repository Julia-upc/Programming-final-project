Python program corresponding to the second part of the final project.

Description

This programme carries out the whole process of generating and analysing investment strategies, in relation to the second part of the final project of the 'Programming for Data Science' course. First of all, the process of generating strategies with their corresponding indicators (return, volatility) is carried out, and then the results are analysed by means of visualisation methods and the like.
